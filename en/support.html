Meet us

We organize our events through Meetup, and keep an activity calendar : check our Calendar page.

Are you ready to step up your commitment to OpenStreetMap in Belgium ?
Then become an official member of our organization, by contacting us at board@osm.be.

Do you just want to be updated about our most important news and activities ?
Then subscribe to our newsletter using this other form.

Support us

Donating to OpenStreetMap Belgium helps our group to host this website, refund travel expenses for volunteers, and it enables us to organize specific projects. We also use the donations to have t-shirts, stickers and banners made for promotion.

You can use the following form or make a wired transfer on Open Knowledge Belgium bank account : {{ site.data.contact.bank }}.
Please add a note to the wire transfer stating that your donation is for OpenStreetMap Belgium.
